At The Hackerati, we are compelled to find true answers to tough questions, and not stop at the first hypothesis that sounds reasonable. We want to bring that passion to customers across many industries who need to create the next generation of whatever they do.

Challenges

Many established companies risk being leapfrogged by smaller, more innovative firms. Some choose to innovate through acquisition, which can effectively mitigate the risks associated with R&D, but often involve paying a premium for an already-developed solution and support organization that then has to be integrated. Many that choose to innovate internally find it hard to focus existing resources on the future when so much depends on meeting short-term objectives this month and this quarter. They usually have to take on a significant capital expense and ongoing fixed cost to build and maintain an internal R&D capability.

Benefits

The Hackerati's variable cost, Research as a Service model offers established and emerging companies, alike, compelling advantages:

  • Effectively mitigate the risk of obsolescence
  • No capital investment or fixed costs
  • We continuously demonstrate value in terms that our clients define, so the risk of ongoing R&D efforts that never produce results is extremely contained
  • Compelling alternative to paying a premium to acquire and integrate another company
