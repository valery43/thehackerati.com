At The Hackerati, we have a passion for building great products, and we want to bring that passion to customers across many industries who need to bring technology products to market.

Challenges

Many companies are having difficulty hiring enough talented engineers to build the sophisticated products that their markets are increasingly demanding, quickly enough to achieve time-to-market goals. Some would prefer to quickly build a minimum viable product to test the product concept in the market before committing to a larger capital investment and taking on substantial fixed costs to support a new business. In a few cases, the initial design and development capacity needed to bring a new product to market are often significantly higher than the capacity needed for ongoing operationof the product.

Benefits

The Hackerati's variable cost Engineering as a Service model offers established and emerging companies, alike, compelling advantages:

  • Rapidly bring new products to market
  • No capital investment or fixed costs
  • We continuously demonstrate value in terms that our clients define, so the risk of ongoing product development efforts that never produce results is extremely contained
  • Compelling alternative to paying a premium to acquire and integrate another company
