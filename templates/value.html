Our unique business model of blending leading experts with experienced engineers and student interns allows us to deliver high-end services at a moderate price, helping businesses address the shortage of engineering talent that they currently face, helping students pay for school and graduate with a year or more of professional engineering experience, and helping academic institutions grow revenue to further their mission of preparing the next generation of engineers for the workforce.

Our standard business arrangements are based on the following components:

Engineering as a Service

  • No Long Term Commitments
  • Monthly performance measurement
  • Ramp up/down with 30 days notice

Time & Materials

  • Competitive, blended, hourly rate
  • Ask about discounts when deliverables are contributed to open source projects

Option to Hire Your Team

  • Competitive fee based on a percentage of the starting annual salary
  • Eligible after six months of working on your project
  • Students eligible only after graduation

Work For Hire

  • You own everything that we produce on your behalf
