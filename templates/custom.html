At The Hackerati, we are more than just skilled technicians. We don't just engineer solutions correctly; we make sure that we engineer the correct solutions. We ask "why" a lot to make sure that we are solving the right problem in the right way.

Challenges

Many engineering projects face an up-hill battle: according to The Standish Group, nearly 1/3 of all IT project fail, and one of the most common causes of failure is an insufficient understanding of the requirements. Stated another way, they might have had a great solution, but they solved the wrong problem!
